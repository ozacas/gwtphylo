1kp AGP & GT2 genes

This website reports major cell wall related gene families, based on data collected by the OneKP consortium, of which the ARC Centre of Excellence in Plant Cell Walls is a gene family expert contributor. This site represents a baseline, or first edition, of genes in families thought to be related to GT2 and/or AGP families and their phylogenetic relationship. In some cases, due to extensive proline-rich repeat regions, accurate phylogenetic reconstruction is difficult. The reader is cautioned to treat these phylogenetic trees as a hypothesis rather than conclusive proof.
